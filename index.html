You control the catalog

Paste a YouTube URL and it's added to your child's approved list. Nothing else can play — not a single recommendation, not a related video.

Daily time limits

Set how many minutes each child can watch per day. The app tracks it automatically and stops playback when the limit is reached.

Time windows

Restrict when the app is usable — for example, only after 3pm on school days. Outside those hours, the app shows a friendly lock screen.

Multiple child profiles

Create separate profiles for each child with their own time limits and catalog. Every child gets exactly the setup they need.

Activity log

See exactly what was watched, when, and for how long. No guessing about how your child's screen time is actually being spent.

No algorithms. Ever.

Getting started

Set up in minutes

No complicated configuration. No child accounts to create. Just add videos and set limits.

  1. 1

    Create your account

    Sign up with your email. You're the parent — your account controls everything.

  2. 2

    Add videos to the catalog

    Paste a YouTube URL. ScreenTime fetches the title and thumbnail automatically. Repeat until the catalog is complete.

  3. 3

    Set up a child profile

    Enter your child's name and set their daily screen time limit. Optionally add time windows — for example, weekdays 4pm–7pm only.

  4. 4

    Hand over the phone

    Switch to Kids Mode. Your child sees only their approved catalog — no search bar, no settings, no way out without your permission.
